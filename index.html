Frequently Asked Questions

常见问题

1. How do I identify a plant?

1. 如何识别植物？

Open the app, tap the camera icon, and take a photo of the plant or select one from your gallery. The AI will identify the plant and provide care tips.

打开应用，点击相机图标，拍摄或选择植物照片，AI会自动识别并提供养护建议。

2. How do I subscribe to premium features?

2. 如何订阅高级功能？

Go to the Subscription Plans page and choose a monthly or yearly plan. Follow the instructions to complete your purchase via the App Store.

进入订阅页面，选择月度或年度套餐，按照提示通过App Store完成购买。

3. Why can't I access the camera or photo library?

3. 为什么无法访问相机或相册？

Please ensure you have granted camera and photo library permissions in your device settings. The app will prompt you if permissions are missing.

请确保已在设备设置中授予相机和相册权限。如未授权，应用会自动弹窗提示。

4. How do I restore my purchases?

4. 如何恢复购买？

On the Subscription Plans page, tap "Restore Purchases" to recover your previous subscriptions.

在订阅页面点击“恢复购买”按钮，即可找回之前的订阅。

5. How do I contact support?

5. 如何联系客服？

If you have any questions or need help, please contact us via email: Intellisant_001@outlook.com

如有疑问或需要帮助，请通过邮箱联系我们：Intellisant_001@outlook.com
